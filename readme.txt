Food Ordering Database Management System is designed to efficiently handle online food ordering operations. It enables the management of customer details, restaurant information, food items, delivery partner details, payments, and order details. The system allows for storing and managing customer data such as names, contact information and order history. Restaurants can be managed, along with their menus and related information. Food items are stored with details like name, description and price. Orders can be tracked, including order details, quantities, delivery status, and timestamps. Payment management is also included, handling payment details and transaction records securely.
Additionally, the system incorporates the management of delivery partners, their names,contact information and performance metrics. The project incorporates an ER diagram, relational model, and normalization techniques to optimize the database structure. The system is built using MySQL.
